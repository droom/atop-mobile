We gave our classic series a twist

Our unique patented world time movement now comes in a variety of colours for every personality.

Made from high grade PC and with an aluminium alloy city bezel to switch to the desired timezone attached to a sweat resistant silicon rubber strap. The hands are copper alloy with luminous functionality for better visibility.

Atop Precision Industries

Atop Precision Industries is a leading manufacturer of watches specialising in the world time function with over 15 years of experience in the watch making business making micro precise parts with its own research and development department to boot.

The inventors, Mark Lin and his wife Grace thought up the concept of a watch with a built in 24 world time zone feature after their major customers had moved production from Taiwan to other production locations.

Their efforts were recognised after 8 years and over a million dollars spent on development costs in the form of the prestigious iF Design Award awarded in 2003 for the technological advancements they achieved. Many awards soon followed including the German Reddot design award, Promotional Gidt award and the Symbol of Excellence Award.

Now their patented leading world time clock and watch is available in a variety of colours, materials and innovative wearable designs are available to you.
